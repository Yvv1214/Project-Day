Singapore

Diversity

Singapore is one of the most multcultural places you'll ever see. With diversities ranging from Chinese to malays, and from Indians to Eurasians. It is also religiously diverse as well. You can find mosques, churches, and temples from a variety of religions such as Buddhism, Hinduism, Christianity, Islam, and Taoism. Being so diverse also means that you can find lots to eat from foods from around the world from a variety of restruants. Also, Singapore has several diverse public holidays such as Easter, Christmas, Chinese New Year, and Vesak. Plan your trip accordingly and you might get to experience foriegn holiday.

Scenery

Singapore has some very intrigueing unique architecture. One of its popular gardens, Gardens by the Bay, contains several supertree grooves which are artificial trees that are about 50 metres tall holding skywalks from one tree to another. There is also the Cloud Forest in a gaint glass house with a forest inside, skywalks, and an indoor waterfall. Aside from the gardens, Singapore also unique buildings from a variety locations with its unique themes. Such as Joo Chiat (a colorfull neighboorhood), Little India, Arab Street, and Chinatown. Even the airport known as Changi, is filled stunning views and lots to do. Changi contains hiking trails, gardens, and a stunningly unique indoor waterfall.

A few more things

While theres alot to do and see and in Singapore, you can lay back and relax at siloso Beach Resort or sunbathe at Palawan beach. You can also resort to the Boat Hotel at Marina Bay Sands. Here you can enjoy a luxurious atmosphere and visit the boat on top of the three buildings containing a restruant and an infinity pool, where you can enjoy the view. If you have the need to shop, Marina Bay Sands also has Shoppes where you can get your shopping needs done and ride on gondolas through the canals inside.
